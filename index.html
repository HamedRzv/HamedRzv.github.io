About

I am a Master of Science student in Information Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, with academic and professional experience at the intersection of data analytics, environmental planning, and sustainability. My work focuses on applying data science, machine learning, and geospatial analysis to real-world problems related to climate, public health, and environmental governance.

Currently, I serve as a Data Extraction and Management Consultant with the Office of Strategic Initiatives at the University of Illinois System, where I develop data pipelines and visualizations for a statewide Climate and Health Matrix. I integrate information from Climate Action Plans and open data portals to evaluate progress on sustainability goals across Illinois counties.

I am passionate about sustainability analytics, urban climate governance, and using data-driven insights to support better environmental decision-making.
